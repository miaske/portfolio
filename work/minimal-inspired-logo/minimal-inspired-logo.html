poskus perspective

Poskus Perspective's goal is to convey simplicity and minimalism while sharing perspectives on the fashion industry through a blog platform.

achieving perfection

The design process didn't stop until satisfaction was achieved. There were multiple stages into achieving the final product.

version 1

My initial thought about going through with a minimal black and white logo to represent fashion, I pictured a design flashy and stylish, while still keeping the simplicity and monotone colors. I designed a basic square abbreviation logo and a full brand name logo.

version 2

The logo needed to be more simplified and more bold. So I took away the geometric lines and went for more bold typography. Then I put a simple black border encasing the text so it wouldn't look too bland, also while still keeping the simplicity in mind.

final version

The typography was too bold and the logo needed to be in the most simplest way it could be. So I got rid of the border in its' entireity, and only focused on the main objective, 'PP'. I picked a thinner font weight and at first the P's weren't overlapping. But then I saw how bland it looked and decided to have them collide so it didn't look too bland, while still keeping the minimalism theme.
